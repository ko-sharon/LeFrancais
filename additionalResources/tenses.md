# Key Tenses

| Priority | Tense | Form (je) | Story flow | English |
|----------|-------|-----------|------------|---------|
| 🥇 | Imparfait | **j'étais** | Avant, j'étais timide. | Before, I used to be shy. |
| 🥇 | Passé composé | **j'ai été** | Hier, j'ai été courageux. | Yesterday, I was brave. |
| 🥇 | Présent | **je suis** | Aujourd'hui je suis content. | Today I am content. |
| 🥇 | Futur proche | **je vais aller** | Ce soir je vais aller à une fête. | Tonight I am going to a party. |
| 🥈 | Futur simple | **je serai** | Demain je serai heureux. | Tomorrow I will be happy. |
| 🥈 | Conditionnel | **je serais** | Je serais encore plus heureux avec un chien! | I would be even happier with a dog! |

## Tenses intentionally left out

- **Plus-que-parfait** (*j'avais été*) — the "past of the past," used to describe something that happened before another past event. Lower priority at this stage; you'll encounter it more in later chapters.
- **Futur antérieur** (*j'aurai été*) — used to describe something that will have happened before a future event. Rarely needed in everyday speech.
- **Subjonctif** (*que je sois*) — triggered by expressions of wish, doubt, or emotion (*je veux que tu sois heureux*). Important eventually but best absorbed through reading exposure rather than memorization.
- **Passé simple** (*je fus*) — a literary past tense found in books and formal writing, not used in spoken French. You will see it in reading but never need to produce it.
